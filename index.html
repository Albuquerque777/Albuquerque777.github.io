Power BI Power Query DAX IT Project Management Procurement Analytics Demand Analytics

Executive analytics project focused on IT project governance, purchasing processes, demand management and data coverage between operational bases. The solution includes three dashboards: Procurement, Demand Management and Procurement x Demand Coverage.
