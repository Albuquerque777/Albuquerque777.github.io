## Dashboard Objective

The Power BI dashboard provides an executive view of customer behavior, consumption patterns, campaign response, segmentation and marketing performance.

The dashboard supports business questions such as:

- What is the customer profile?
- Which customers spend the most?
- Which segments have higher business potential?
- What is the campaign response rate?
- Which customer groups should be prioritized?
- How can marketing campaigns become more efficient?

## Suggested Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Main KPIs and overall business performance.

Recommended visuals:

- Total customers
- Campaign response rate
- Average spending
- Total revenue proxy
- Average income
- Average age

### 2. Campaign Performance

Campaign response analysis and targeting insights.

Recommended visuals:

- Response rate
- Campaign responders vs non-responders
- Response by income group
- Response by customer segment
- Propensity score distribution

### 3. Customer Segmentation

Customer segments generated through Python clustering.

Recommended visuals:

- Segment size
- Average spending by segment
- Average recency by segment
- Segment action plan

### 4. Consumption Analysis

Customer spending and product category behavior.

Recommended visuals:

- Spending by category
- Spending by age group
- Spending by income group
- Purchase channel analysis

### 5. Executive Action Plan

Business pains, recommended actions and success KPIs.

Recommended visuals:

- Business pain matrix
- Recommended CRM actions
- KPIs to monitor
- Priority groups

## Business Value

The dashboard translates analytical outputs into executive decision-making views, supporting marketing prioritization, customer segmentation, campaign optimization and CRM strategy.
